About the Band

Born from the ashes of apocalyptic noise, Hopes Ignite blend melodic metalcore with cinematic storytelling. Their latest release Hollow Vessel explores the fight to keep humanity’s heart beating in a crumbling world.

Bringing back music from the Southern California millennial scene. Creating our vision of a moment lost in time; Raw, melodic, heavy music focused on a love for our community and for each other. Our debut album "Hollow Vessel" is in the works. The album will be released on vinyl and all streaming services. Shows to follow...

Follow our friends in @wildlikewolves with members of Hopes Ignite; a project with a similar vision led by our good friend Gary Cuellar. His new EP is out now, go check it out...
